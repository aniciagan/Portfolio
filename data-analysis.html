Data Cleaning

For this project, I will import and clean data from the following key sources to ensure it is accurate and ready for analysis.

1. Historical COE Data

The most comprehensive and up-to-date data on COE bidding is available from the Land Transport Authority (LTA) through data.gov.sg.

  • COE Bidding Results / Prices: This dataset provides historical COE prices, quota, and bids received for each bidding exercise and vehicle category. Link
  • Motor Vehicle Quota, Quota Premium And Prevailing Quota Premium, Monthly: This is another excellent dataset from SingStat that also provides a longer history dating back to 2002. Link

2. Motor Vehicle Population Data

The LTA also provides detailed statistics on the motor vehicle population, including new registrations and de-registrations.

  • Annual Motor Vehicle Population by Vehicle Type: This dataset provides the total number of registered vehicles broken down by type (e.g., cars, motorcycles, taxis) on a yearly basis. Link
  • LTA Statistics page: This page is a great resource for a wider range of vehicle statistics, including monthly and yearly breakdowns of registration, de-registration, and population figures. Link

3. Key Economic Indicators

For economic data, the primary source is the Department of Statistics (DOS) Singapore, also known as SingStat.

  • SingStat Website: This is the central hub for all official Singapore statistics. You can search for specific indicators like GDP, inflation (Consumer Price Index), and household income. Link
  • Key Economic Indicators - Ministry of Trade and Industry (MTI): The MTI website provides high-level economic indicators and reports, which can be useful for a general overview. Link
