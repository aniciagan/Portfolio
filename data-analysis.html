Case Study / Project Example

Analyzing Customer Churn for a SaaS Product

Problem: A SaaS company was experiencing a high churn rate, but the reasons were unclear. They needed to identify key factors contributing to churn and actionable insights to reduce it.

Solution:

  • Extracted customer usage data, subscription history, and support ticket logs from various databases using SQL.
  • Cleaned and preprocessed the data using Python (Pandas library), handling missing values and outliers.
  • Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify initial correlations and patterns, visualizing distributions and relationships with Matplotlib and Seaborn.
  • Conducted statistical tests (e.g., t-tests, chi-squared) to determine significant differences between churned and active users.
  • Built a predictive churn model using a classification algorithm (e.g., Logistic Regression or Random Forest) to identify customers at high risk of churning.
  • Presented findings and actionable recommendations (e.g., targeting specific user segments with retention campaigns, improving onboarding for new users) through interactive Tableau dashboards to stakeholders.

Impact: The analysis identified that users who did not complete the initial onboarding tutorial within 3 days had a 30% higher churn rate. Based on this, the company revamped its onboarding process, leading to a 15% reduction in monthly churn over the next quarter.
