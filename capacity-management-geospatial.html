Project: Capacity Management - GeoSpatial

💢 **Problem:** The client faced challenges with complex geospatial inventory management. Tracking and allocating resources across various geographical locations was inefficient, leading to potential misallocations and delays.

💡 **Solution:** I led a cross-functional team to address this challenge. Our approach involved:

  • Conducting extensive **user research** to understand the pain points and needs of different user personas (e.g., Sales, Operations, Capacity Planning).
  • Gathering detailed **requirements** from various stakeholders to define the scope of a streamlined inventory capacity process.
  • Documenting comprehensive **user stories** that articulated the features from the perspective of the end-users.
  • Facilitating communication and alignment between business, technical, and sales teams to ensure the solution met diverse needs.
  • Analyzing the impact of the project across various departments, including its ultimate effect on customer satisfaction and sales efficiency.

📜 **Tools & Methodologies:** Agile, Jira, Confluence, SQL, API testing (Bruno).

My Contribution & Impact

In this project, my primary role involved translating complex geospatial challenges into actionable technical requirements. By deeply engaging with various departments, I ensured that the developed solutions not only addressed immediate operational inefficiencies but also supported strategic business goals related to sales and customer fulfillment. This included defining data flows for geospatial information and collaborating closely with development teams to ensure accurate implementation of capacity logic.
