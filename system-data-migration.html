Project: System / Data Migration

💢 Problem: After a significant system upgrade or migration to a NextGen platform, a critical challenge emerged: ensuring seamless and accurate consumption of the new data by essential downstream applications. Any discrepancies could lead to operational disruptions and incorrect reporting.

💡 Solution: I played a key role in ensuring data integrity and usability post-migration through the following efforts:

  • Developed an SQL-based data quality dashboard specifically designed for post-migration health monitoring. This dashboard provided real-time visibility into data accuracy, consistency, and completeness, allowing for quick identification and resolution of any issues.
  • Collaborated with data engineers and source system owners to understand the nuances of data transformation and integration.
  • Documented the new data consumption and processing workflows in a detailed Functional Requirements Document (FRD). This FRD served as a crucial guide for technical teams and provided a clear understanding of how downstream systems would interact with the migrated data.
  • Conducted thorough data validation checks and worked with QA teams to design and execute tests specifically for data integrity.
  • Provided support for data reconciliation and troubleshooting, ensuring all critical applications were functioning correctly with the new data.

📜 Tools & Methodologies: FRD, SQL, Data Dashboards, Data Validation.

My Contribution & Impact

In this migration project, my focus was on the critical "last mile" of data quality and usability. By building the SQL-based dashboard and meticulously documenting the new data processes in the FRD, I enabled proactive monitoring and ensured that business users and downstream systems could confidently rely on the newly migrated data. This significantly reduced post-migration issues and fostered trust in the new system's data integrity.
